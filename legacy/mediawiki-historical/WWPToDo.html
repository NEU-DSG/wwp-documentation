Guide Development[edit]

  • Overhaul keywords
  • Finish adding missing content (Technical Advice and Magic sections)
  • Identify and add new articles and entries needed
  • Expand glossary

WWPDoc Development[edit]

  • Clone the Guide architecture
  • Convert current WWP markup doc to Guide P5 schema and substitute into the cloned Guide architecture
  • Fix keywords (80% complete)
  • Overhaul content to separate instructions from rationale; separate sections for P4 and P5 (following Nov. 2008 staff discussion, decided new documentation will only include P5 instructions)
  • Identify and resolve all questions and ambiguities in current P4 encoding, and document these (again, no longer an issue due to P5-only nature of updated documentation)
  • Identify variances within current/historical WWP encoding practice and flag for attention when we convert to P5
