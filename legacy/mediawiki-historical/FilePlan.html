New navigational structure for WWP site[edit]

NB this is not necessarily the actual file hierarchy. The top-level index pages are what you get when you click on the permanently visible navigational button in each area. The second-level items are all visible in drop-down menus (as in CDS site) once you click and hold on that button.

Permanently along the bottom of the page we would also have links for Contact, CDS, and maybe Announcements (but maybe this latter is unnecessary--just needs to be visible on the home page)

  • Top-level page content
    • WWP-related twitter feeds
    • Visualization of textbase (rotating)
    • (more)
  • WWO
    • Index page describes WWO and has links with descriptions to the other items in this area
    • WWO direct link
    • Licensing and trials
    • New and forthcoming
    • WWO Lab
    • Teaching and learning
      • Exhibits
      • Syllabi
  • Research and Publications
    • Index page describes our overall research agenda, with links to other items in this area
    • Projects (grant\- and self-funded)
      • Index page lists all current projects, divider or link at bottom to inactive projects page
      • Projects that require a directory structure of their own would have that here
    • Collaborations
      • Index page lists all current collaborations, including things like MME, Mandala, Orlando
    • Publications
      • Index page lists all types of publications, links to pages below and to any other publications (e.g. exhibits?) that may live elsewhere)
      • Guide
      • Documentation
      • Research library (duplicate CDS content or link to it?)
      • Grant reports
      • Presentations


  • Outreach and Education
    • Index page describes what we mean by "outreach and education" and describes our various activities in this area, with links to the other items in this area; include here link to subscribe to WWP-L and WWP-Encoding
    • Workshops and seminars
    • Women in the Archives
      • Index page describes the conference series with current call/invitation at the top, links to specific events
    • Resources page listing all slide sets, handouts, tutorials, documentation, etc.
    • Consultation
    • Internships


  • About
    • Index page describes the WWP mission (hence maybe no need for a separate mission page?) and links to other items in this area
    • Mission
    • History
      • Link to Archive
    • People
      • Link to Alumni
    • Programs (description with links)
    • Events (list with link to old events?)
