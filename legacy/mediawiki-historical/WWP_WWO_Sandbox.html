Overview[edit]

The sandbox was envisaged as a space where WWP staff and their friends and collaborators could try out new technologies and ways of doing things. The initial space has been set up here:

http://golf.services.brown.edu/sandbox/index.html

and one or two sub-spaces added in the menu. But time and life have intervened and the sandbox is currently dormant.

Architecture[edit]

The sandbox has been constructed as a simple, relatively framework-neutral space, the idea being that disparate experiments can live next to each other without everything having to conform to one system. The index page menu does depend on the jQuery library being present.

User-created markup (currently defunct)[edit]

Intended as a space for testing how users can create, store, and view "Trojan HORSE" markup. We got it 90% working but a show-stopping bug still remains, so the menu link has been removed from the index.html page for now.
